Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What types of projects do you work on?

We specialize in spatial analysis, design, and community engagement projects. This includes GIS analysis, map creation, document design, logo development, stakeholder facilitation, and public participation processes.

How long do projects typically take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple design projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive spatial analysis or community engagement projects can take 3-8 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Yes, we work with clients across the United States and internationally. We're experienced in remote collaboration and can adapt our processes to work effectively with distributed teams.

What is your typical project process?

Our process includes: 1) Discovery and consultation, 2) Project planning and proposal, 3) Analysis and development, 4) Review and iteration, and 5) Final delivery and support. We maintain regular communication throughout each phase.
